## Question
Which paid capabilities should come first once the free local-first MVP is in
place?

## Context
The team agrees that the local-first MVP should remain free, but the exact paid
sequencing still needs product judgment.

## Possible Directions
- account-linked workspace attach and sync across machines
- hosted backup and restore for Memora workspaces
- team approvals, shared review dashboards, and audit history
- import and backfill workflows for large legacy repositories
- advisory semantic discovery and small-model retrieval helpers

## Resolution
Deferred. Paid expansion should start with convenience and collaboration before
it expands into richer advisory intelligence.
