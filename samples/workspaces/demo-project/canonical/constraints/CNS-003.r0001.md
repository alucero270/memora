## Constraint
Milestone 10 must expose deterministic retrieval, proposal submission, and
outcome recording through the shared Memora-facing contract. It must not fork
business logic by vendor as a first move.

## Why It Exists
Memora's leverage comes from shared governed project memory, not from separate
agent adapters that drift over time.

## Implications
Codex may be the first operational client, but M10 packet design still has to
stay compatible with ChatGPT read/validation workflows and future Claude
adoption.
